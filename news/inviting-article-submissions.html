Call for Blog submissions - Article Showcase Contest

We invite students to contribute articles in the AI-ML and programming domains for our blog page. We are looking for fresh insights, any findings, and thoughtful analyses that engage our community.

Participants from our previous ML Challenge editions with decent, effective model scores can consider writing articles on their methodology itself. This would be interesting! We encourage you all to do this.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Goes without saying, content must be original i.e. written by you and free from any copyright claims by third parties. No plagiarism pls, urgh!
  • Articles should not have been posted elsewhere.
  • Please ensure that the work is free from AI-generated content (like GPT etc).
  • Unfortunately, we will accept blogs written in English this time.
  • A student can make one submission only.
  • It should be crisp, max. number of pages can be 3. Cite references wherever applicable.
  • Acceptance to showcase a blog on our site depends on the initiative core team and whatever decided is final. No queries to be entertained about this.
  • If the submitted article is accepted from our end, the same cannot be posted/shared in other platforms.

    • Accepted articles will be featured in our newsletter, making it a special highlight! We look forward to the exceptional quality of your submissions.

    Deadline: 25th November 2024 IST
