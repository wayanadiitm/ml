F.A.Q

  • What's the eligibility to participate?

    This is a PAN BS event. All students (irrespective of the level) enrolled (i.e. having the student mail id) in the IITM UG Data Science or Electronic Systems programme are welcome to register for our event.

  • I'm a foundational student. Should I take part in this contest?

    Absolutely! We strongly encourage you to register. We have a separate category for the Foundational students in the prelims - so you are competing with your fellow mates who're at the same level as you, providing a fair and challenging environment.
    The beauty of LogicLoom 2.0 lies in its accessibility. You don't need prior knowledge of AI and ML to participate. The difficulty level will be tailored to the topics covered in FL courses, ensuring that every participant feels comfortable yet challenged. The key to success lies in your ability to think outside the box and approach problems with creativity and logic. So, don't hesitate - seize the opportunity and go for it!

  • I am unable to access the registration form. What to do?

    Registration will only be possible online via IITM Paradox portal. Please try signing using the IITM student account only.
    If you are facing issues in registering for our event on the Paradox website, please communicate with the Paradox team directly at webops@iitmparadox.org with a CC to us.

  • I can't login to the LogicLooM Participant app portal.

    To access the participants portal and attempt the online rounds, a seamless login system is now enabled through Google OAuth integration. You don't need to remember any creds.
    Simply sign in using the student G-account used while registering for our event on the Paradox portal. If you attempt to log in with a different account, you'll receive an error message and won't be eligible to access it.
    Please write to logicloom@ds.study.iitm.ac.in for any queries.

  • Due to some issues in my internet connectivity I couldn't submit the prelims. Will it be fine?

    Absolutely, there's nothing to worry. The responses marked and saved before the glitch occured, will be evaluated by the auto-grader once the due time passes for your profile. It will be considered as a proper submission, provided all the integrity checks pass once processed by the system (Once the exam ends, all activity logs from the portal will be processed via our system. The entire process is automated. Any cases of discrepancies, mismatch, UFM will be tracked by our tool and will result in the failed checks, followed by the cancellation of your attempt)
    Also, for any loading issues on the question screen (due to server load etc.), don't logout. Just press the refresh icon on the question screen.

  • I logged in to the LogicLooM portal at 7PM. Is that my start time?

    No. Once you login, you will land on the waiting lounge where you can view the quiz related instructions. There should be a 'Launch' button which will get auto-activated once the contest gets live.
    Clicking this button will only start the quiz and take you to the main questions page, which will be considered as your start time and you need to submit within 40 mins from then. Please refer to the live timer on the portal.

  • I started the prelims at 23:45PM. Will I still get 40 minutes?

    No. You will have to finish on or before 23:59 IST (after which the attempt window will close). To utilise the complete duration, you need to start the assessment latest by 23.20 IST. Otherwise, you’ll get less time to answer.
    Please go through the rule book properly.

  • Can I take prelims in both the slots?

    No. Once you start the exam, you have to complete it within the slot in the specified time limit. The maximum number of attempts will be set to 1.
    Moreover, we have provided a flexible window of 28 hours and you can attempt the round for any 40 minutes in this time-frame. Decide your convenient slot wisely. Please check the 'Important Dates' section on the website.

  • I have some other questions that haven't been discussed here.

    We suggest you to read the event Rule book (logicloom.iitmparadox.org/rules) first, as everything has been explained there in detail.
    Still, if you have any general questions or face technical issues, feel free to contact the organizers via mail at logicloom@ds.study.iitm.ac.in. We will try to reply back within one working day.
    You may also contact our Public relations member at +91 9007429038.
