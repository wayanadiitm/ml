Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously and we are committed in protecting the information shared with us. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our event website.

We receive your name, email and whatsapp number (as per submitted by you) only via the event registration form. No other personal or sensitive information is asked by us and we strongly suggest everyone to refrain from sharing any such details. The name and email are neccessary for the event records. The whatsapp number is collected for the purpose of verification while allowing you entry into the event Whatsapp group & will be used as one of the login credentials. The data remains only within us and the fest team and is never shared with anyone else.
Cookies: We are not using any cookies on this (logicloom.netlify.app) website. But, since the contest would be hosted via the dedicated, secure 'online exam management system' portal which is hosted on 000webhost (powered by Hostinger), there are various proctoring tools or features enabled in the portal to track user activities on the website through out the exam time and ensure highest possible exam integrity and track any cases of unfair means (UFM) adopted by any participant. Cookies may be enabled for a better exam experience and can also include your browser type, device IP. Further, you can read the privacy policy of the 000webhost hosting service.

We implement highest possible methods to protect the information shared with us, from any kinds of un-authorised access, disclosure, use etc. The data remains secure with us. Weeding policy: We store or retain the data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected. After 7 days from the end of the event, all event records, including name, email, whatsapp number, scores, exam monitoring and activity logs, etc. are permanently deleted from the exam server.

We may modify the privacy policy anytime without any prior notice, but the same shall reflect on this page, if any. The access or use of our website or features, automatically indicates your acceptance of the privacy policy. Incase you have any questions about the same, feel free to contact us.
