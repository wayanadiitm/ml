Instructions

Participants from our previous ML Challenge editions with decent, effective model scores can consider writing articles on their methodology, findings (regarding techniques used, what improved the performance, model architecture etc.) itself. This would be interesting! We encourage you all to do this. If you had participated as a team back then, you can collaborate to write, as well.

Submission Guidelines
1. Goes without saying, content must be original i.e. written by you and free from any copyright claims by third parties. No plagiarism pls, urgh!
2. Articles should not have been posted elsewhere.
3. Please ensure that the work is free from AI-generated content (like GPT etc).
4. Regrettably, English will be the only language accepted this time.
5. You can make one submission only. You can revise and submit it anytime before deadline.
6. It should be crisp, try to align with this page limit - max. number of pages can be 3. Cite references wherever applicable.
7. Acceptance to showcase a blog on our site depends on the initiative core team and whatever decided is final. No queries to be entertained about this.
8. If the submitted article is approved from our end, the same cannot be posted/shared in other platforms.
Shortlisted articles will be featured in our newsletter, making it a special highlight! We look forward to the exceptional quality of your submissions.
