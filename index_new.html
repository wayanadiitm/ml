About Us

We launched our initiative in Wayanad in October 2023, and it has been met with overwhelming love, success, and positive feedback. We've conducted 2 mega-events at Paradox Technicals that became one of the celebrated events among students with more than 1000+ unique participants combined and Profs. from IITGN, IITR and IITM course instructors being a part of it.

We would like to express our heartfelt gratitude to Prof. Sudarshan, Prof. Jyothi, Mr. Piyush, the Paradox Team and IITM Student Affairs for their unwavering support always.
