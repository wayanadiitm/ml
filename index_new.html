About Us

We launched our initiative in Wayanad in October 2023, and it has been met with overwhelming love, success, and positive feedback. We've conducted 2 mega-events at Paradox Technicals that became one of the celebrated events among students with more than 1000+ unique participants combined and Profs. from IITGN, IITR and IITM course instructors being a part of it.

We would like to express our heartfelt gratitude to Prof. Sudarshan, Prof. Jyothi, Mr. Piyush, the Paradox Team and IITM Student Affairs for their unwavering support always.

Frequently Asked Questions

I'm a fresher. Should I join this community?

Definitely! That's the main objective of the initiative. We provide easy-to-understand resources and a detailed roadmap to help you get familiar with Python first. Our goal is for you to gain a solid understanding of the basics and how ML works.

I'm not from Wayanad. Can I join?

Sure! This initiative is for BS students across all houses.

Is there a fee to participate?

Not at all. All our events are absolutely free!

What topics are usually covered?

We cover Python basics for beginners, as well as topics in ML and NLP domains. Our events emphasize logic and creativity throughout the series.
