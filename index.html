We've conducted 2 mega-events at Paradox Technicals that became one of the celebrated events among students with more than 1000+ unique participants combined and Profs. from IITGN, IITR and IITM course instructors being a part of it.

To be Updated
You need not worry if you're just starting out and don't have a technical or coding background. We’re providing you with easy-to-understand resources and a detailed roadmap to help you get familiar with Python first. For anyone following this roadmap dedicatedly, these will be the pre-requisites to get started with ML basics.
Honestly, we don't expect you to jump into complex libraries, train and fine-tune transformers or surpass the benchmark scores. We understand that it's not possible since you're completely new here and this requires ML mastery to understand the nuances in the dataset, what models will best suit the task etc.

Our goal isn’t for you to master these intricate aspects right away, but rather to gain a solid understanding of the basics and how ML works.
As students of DS, you eventually need to dive into ML and this will be a fantastic hands-on opportunity & learning experience. Give it a try. Learn, join our sessions and enjoy the events :)
